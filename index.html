1. Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or home address.

However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information when you use the App.

Automatically Collected Data

The following categories of data may be collected:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Identifiers (such as IDFA or IDFV, where permitted)
  • App usage data (game progress, interaction events, session duration)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Approximate location (country or region level)

We use this data solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Fix crashes and technical issues
  • Provide relevant advertising

We do not sell your personal data.
